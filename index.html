ABOUT MATTHEW

Matthew is a student in the integrated Master in Professional Accounting program at McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin. He is currently pursuing his goals of being an accounting/finance professional in the consulting and advisory spaces, with a long-term orientation towards digital transformation and business analytics solutions.

Through his experiences at a Big 4 accounting firm, a Fortune 25 downstream refining company, and a Fortune 100 construction equipment manufacturer, among others, he has been able to apply his various technical skills and soft skills to create meaningful, effective projects and gain exposure to modernized ERP systems and automated business processes. He is very interested in the versatility of business and the many ways it is being enhanced through technology, and he is excited to continue to round out his skills as a future business leader.

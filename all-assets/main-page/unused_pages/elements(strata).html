ABOUT MATTHEW

Matthew is a student in the integrated Master in Professional Accounting program at McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin.

He is currently pursuing his interests within accounting, finance, and business in general, and he holds valuable real-world experience in multiple corporate business environments. This includes some local oilfield services companies, a Fortune 50 downstream refining company, and a Fortune 100 construction equipment manufacturer. He is highly skilled in MS Excel, has experience with ERP software (NetSuite, QuickBooks Online, and SAP), and understands what it means to lead ethically and effectively. He is very interested in the versatility of business and is excited to continue to round out his skills as a future business leader.

ABOUT MATTHEW

Matthew is a recent graduate of the integrated Master in Professional Accounting program at McCombs School of Business at The University of Texas at Austin. He is currently pursuing his goals of being an accounting/finance professional in the consulting and advisory spaces, with a long-term orientation towards digital transformation and business strategy.

Matthew has professional experiences with both large and small enterprises in a range of diverse business areas. Notably, these have been in commodity trading, audit and assurance services, digital transformation, and traditional finance functions, among other areas. He is very interested in the versatility of business and the many ways in which technology can be used to enhance the processes, operations, and overall management of an organization. He is excited to continue to round out his skills as a future business leader and to, ultimately, create a future worth striving for.

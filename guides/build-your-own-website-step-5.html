You now have a fully functioning site that you can share to wherever your heart desires!

If you were expecting an actual Step 5 and/or beyond, well, there isn't one because from here it is up to you! So whether you stop here and just share it with your friends and on social media, or if you decide to explore web development further and really flesh-out your website and more, you have officially published your first basic webpage to the internet. Congratulations!

At this point you should have a working site, but if you are having any major issues with this or are unable to get your site to appear (or if you have any other questions or comments), send a message below and and we can work through it together!
